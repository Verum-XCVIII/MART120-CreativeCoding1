Short Story

The shortest story, is no story at all.

There once was a man delivered the arduous task of creating a short story, but a story writer, he was not. This burden weighed heavy upon him for countless days and nights, as he pondered what he could possibly write about. He searched high and low throughout all the lands in search for even the faintest hint of creativity. But alas, his search yielded no results, for it seemed all the land had run dry and barren of creativity. Some say he is still aimlessly wandering the earth to this day, searching for this elusive creativity, for a creative writer, he is not.
